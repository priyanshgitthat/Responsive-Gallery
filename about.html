about me

priyansh verma

Remember those bulky computers from back in the day? Yeah, those were the ones that sparked my curiosity about the magic behind the screen. Now, juggling college (shoutout to MCA!) and building websites keeps me on my toes. You could say I'm a coding ninja – quiet and focused when the code editor's open, but with the skills to build awesome websites that come alive. Python's my trusty programming language, but.....

I can speak C and Java fluently too. This website you're checking out? Built it myself, with some serious late-night coding sessions!

But coding isn't my only superpower. When I'm not battling lines of code, I'm a weekend warrior, diving headfirst into epic movies (think superheroes with mind-blowing twists and turns!). That's where the "creative alchemist" part kicks in. See, I don't just watch movies, I dissect them – the visuals, the pacing, the way they tell a story. And that passion translates into my video and photo editing. I love using color grading and smooth transitions to transform videos from raw footage into captivating stories. With photos, I'm all about bringing out the hidden details, whether it's sharpening landscapes to capture the majesty of nature or making portraits glow with a touch of digital magic.

So, if you're looking for a web developer who's a coding whiz by day and a creative explorer on weekends, let's connect! I'm always up for a challenge, a good chat about movies (be prepared for some epic recommendations!), or maybe even a brainstorming session to breathe life into your next website project. After all, the best websites are the ones that combine technical skills with a sprinkle of creative flair, right?
